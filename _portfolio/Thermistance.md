Thermistance Technologies are pioneers in passive cooling solutions through their proprietary miniaturized loop heat pipe technology and in house heat pipe manufacturing. Thermistance specialises in cooling systems for power electronics, data centers and EVs without the use of fans or pumped liquid cooling, this enables them to protect these systems from dust and humidity while also reducing the energy consumption burden for system cooling
