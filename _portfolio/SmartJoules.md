Smart Joules is a New Delhi-based energy-efficiency technology company building a scalable, asset-light platform to deliver profitable decarbonisation across commercial and industrial buildings in India. The company offers a full-stack solution that includes design, implementation, and continuous optimisation of efficient HVAC and building-automation systems, enabled by its proprietary IoT and AI platform, DeJoule.

Through its flagship offerings, JoulePAYS (Pay-as-you-Save retrofits) and JouleCOOL (Cooling as a Service), Smart Joules helps clients reduce energy consumption by 20–30% while requiring no upfront capital. Their model aligns incentives between the company and its customers by linking revenues directly to verified energy savings, de-risking adoption and accelerating efficiency deployment at scale.

With India’s growing demand for cooling and pressure on the power grid, Smart Joules addresses a large and urgent market opportunity in energy efficiency. Its recurring-revenue, data-driven platform approach positions it to achieve both attractive unit economics and measurable climate impact, making it a strong fit for investors seeking scalable, impact-aligned infrastructure and climate-tech solutions.
