As an Investment and Impact Management Analyst at Synapses, Iwank work focuses on the evaluation of the environmental consequences of investments. His commitment is towards the development of tools that can be used to monitor and report on the environmental impacts of early-stage start-ups, facilitating an informed decision-making culture within them.

​<br>
Iwank holds a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering from Sam Higginbottom University of Agriculture, Technology & Sciences, and a Master's degree in Disaster Management from Tata Institute of Social Sciences. Additionally, Iwank is a Climate change Fellow from Terra. Do and holds a diploma in Urban Studies from the Indian Institute for Human Settlements.

​<br>
Iwank loves to skateboard, go rock climbing, and sway people with his impeccable vocal skills in karaoke.
